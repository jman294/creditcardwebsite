The Pros & Cons

What are Credit Cards?

Credit cards allow you to borrow money on a temporary basis. When you spend a credit card, you are taking out a loan that you will have to pay back.

These are the major credit card companies

What are pros of credit cards?

  • They are convenient for safer purchases online and in person
  • They can build up your credit score
  • Fraud protection
  • Come with rewards and promotions
Most end up having a credit card during their lifetimes for their advantages, but they can be dangerous tools.

What are cons of credit cards?

  • You can spend more money than you should
  • Interest and hidden fees add up very quickly
  • Your debt can grow very quickly
  • Credit cards can be confusing or hostile

Some stats

  • US credit card debt is over 1 trillion dollars
  • It could take a decade to pay off the average houshold credit card debt of $5,700
  • Over half of Americans have credit card debt
